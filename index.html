Basic Text Tags

When choosing a tag pair...

  • p – paragraph text
  • em – emphasized text
  • strong – important text
  • cite – title of a work
  • mark – highlighted text
  • b – attention text
  • i – alternate voice
  • u – underlined
  • kbd – keyboard input
  • samp – sample output
  • var – variable

Grouping and Describing Content

Headings help structure content...

  • h1 – main heading
  • h2 – sub-topic
  • h3 – smaller sub-topic
  • section – container for related content

Special Characters & Lists

These are common entity characters:

  • © – copyright
  •   – non-breaking space
  • & – ampersand
  • < and > – less/greater than

List tags:

  • ol – ordered list
  • ul – unordered list
  • li – list item
